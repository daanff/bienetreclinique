Sciatica

Pain radiating along the sciatic nerve — complementary care can help manage discomfort and promote muscular relaxation.

Common signs

  • Pain starting in the lower back or buttock
  • Radiation along the thigh, sometimes to the foot
  • Tingling or numbness in the leg
  • Worse with walking, sitting or bending
  • Associated lower back stiffness

Our TCM approach

Sciatica requires careful assessment. In TCM, care focuses on unblocking stagnation along affected meridians, tonifying deficient areas and releasing lumbar and pelvic tension. Acupuncture is often combined with targeted massage and, when appropriate, cupping or gua sha. We also welcome SAAQ and CNESST files when treatment is authorized.

This information is for educational purposes only and does not replace advice from a healthcare professional. Results vary from person to person.

Recommended care

  • Classic acupuncture
  • Deep tissue massage
  • Cupping
  • Gua sha
